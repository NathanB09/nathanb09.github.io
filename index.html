About

My whole life, I’ve been a builder. Whether that’s something physical or in tech, I enjoy creating something from nothing and see a tangible, physical result emerge. Coding lets me be creative, with no set way of accomplishing any objective. I am passionate about writing clean, easy-to-read code, and I can be a little OCD about it. What drives me to being a developer is the continuous cycle of learning something new and putting that knowledge to practice.

I’m particularly enjoying developing my skills on the Front End at the moment, with Javascript as my current favourite language and React.js as a framework. I am also eager to learn Node.js for application in the backend. I have experience with creating API’s in Ruby on Rails and I’m refreshing my skills in Python as well.

Because I’ve lived in three countries in my life, I feel I bring an unique perspective and understanding when it comes to being in new environments and cultures. It has allowed me to see the differences in culture and the way people live and I have become comfortable with that.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy weight training, climbing/bouldering, playing guitar, and playing video games.
